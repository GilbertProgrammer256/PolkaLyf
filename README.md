The project combines modern frontend and backend technologies to deliver a seamless experience for patients, healthcare providers, and stakeholders.

## About

PolkaLyf aims to modernize healthcare by using **Web3 and blockchain** to ensure:

- **Secure patient data management**  
- **Transparent medical records**  
- **Decentralized healthcare transactions**  
- **Efficient communication between patients and providers**  

By integrating blockchain, the platform reduces fraud, enhances privacy, and empowers patients with control over their health data.

---

## Features

- **Patient Management:** Track and store patient records securely on the blockchain.  
- **Healthcare Provider Dashboard:** Manage appointments, records, and services.  
- **Decentralized Payments:** Web3-based payment system for healthcare services.  
- **Immutable Medical Records:** Records cannot be altered or tampered with.  
- **User Authentication:** Secure login using blockchain identity or traditional methods.
